About Me!

Hello, I'm Rizwan Khalid, a driven and ambitious student specializing in Remote Sensing and GIS with a strong focus on Geo Informatics and web development. Currently pursuing my Bachelor's degree, I'm excited about the potential at the intersection of geospatial technology and the web to create cutting-edge solutions. I have developed expertise in image classification, land cover mapping, and spatial analysis, along with proficiency in data processing techniques like object-based image analysis and digital elevation modeling. Passionate about leveraging geospatial data to address pressing issues, I am dedicated to closing the gap between remote sensing, GIS, and web development.

In web development, I create user-friendly interfaces using HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. With an eye for design, I strive to deliver seamless and aesthetically pleasing websites. From land cover categorization using satellite imagery to interactive online maps for spatial data visualization, I bridge the gap between these disciplines. I'm always eager to learn about new technologies and stay updated in my field. Seeking opportunities to collaborate with individuals and organizations that share my passion for geospatial data and web development, I would be delighted to connect and discuss partnerships or provide more information about my work.
